(i. moZRvriSvilis „uZvelesi qarTuli gvar-saxelebidan“, (gv. 1027), 
mopovebuli tuRuSis stambis masalebidan, q. foTi, XIX saukune)
CxaiZe – erT-erTi uZvelesi da yvelaze didi gvaria saqarTveloSi (aigiveben maTTan Caxavebsa da bevr sxvas).
aris ramdenime legenda-versia gvaris warmoqmnisa:
1. `Cxa~ – karg meurne kacs vazis mtevnebis didi akidoebi saxlis erdoze savsed da jgrod gamoukidia (albaT saCamiCed), Sedegad SeerqvaT CxaiZeebi... (amboben, vazis xnobidan modiso es gvari).
2. Zveli qarTuli (warmarTuli droidanve) tradiciiT moklul mters marjvena xelis mtevans `Sexsnidnen~. aseTi `mtevnebis~ `Cxa~-Ti kai ymas (vaJkacs) saxlis erdo jgrod da saCinod hqonda damSvenebuli. aqac Sedegad `Cxa~-iZe.
... sazogadoebrivi formaciebis Sesabamisi iyo aborigeni mosaxleobis tradiciebic: _ kai yma Tavis droze karg mekobres, abrags, firals da ase Semdeg Seerqmeoda da maT qceva-yofas Sesabamisi `atributika amSvenebda~...
aris sxva `Tqmulebanic~ gvaris warmoSobisa, magram isini Zalze `xelovnurad~ aRiqmebian (aradamajereblad)...
erTi istoriac:
ese CxaiZe – daviT aRmaSeneblis samefo karis qronikebis masalebSi mefis msaxurionis gansakuTrebuli uflebamosilebis warCinebulad moixsenieba...
samefo karis msaxurionis es gavleniani kasta SemadgenlobiT yovelTvis Wrelia – mefis gansakuTrebul samsaxurSi CarTuli didebulni da aznaurni Tu Sualedi (Tavisufali) fenebidan dawinaurebulni da zogjer yvelaze maRla aRzevebulnic...
msaxurionTa kastis ZiriTadi birTvia mefis madevarni (sxva wyaroebiT – mandaturnic) – sayrdeni samefos Zlieri saSinao Tu sagareo politikisa.
am arenaze moixsenieba ese CxaiZe – kaci Zlieri, friad gamWriaxi gonebis, medgari, vervisgan damcrobili, bunebiT kuSti, dRemudam mefesTan mdgari, ojaxad – naadrevad qvrivi, mama Svidi vaJiSvilisa: udedod izrdebodnen SemZle da saxelovan ojaxSi simamr-sidedrTan...
samefo karzeve iyo ufrosi Zma esesi – manase xucesi, friad pativdebuli, ganaTlebuli, mwignobari (uSvilZiro)...
... moxda ubedureba – nadirobisas brma SemTxvevam imsxverpla mefis karTan erTob daaxloebuli didebuli baram _ warmomadgeneli orbelTa gavleniani gvarisa; Turme misi lamazi qvrivi, rogorc gvian gairkva, eses nayvareba SoriT. agorda Wori: qals ukve mowifuli qal-vaJi darCa, _ ojaxi gailanZRa da udanaSaulo qvrivi monazvnad aRikveca, (Tu aRkveces!) monasterSi...
... glovis erTi wlis Tavze ese daeTxova samefo kars, Tavisi adgil-mamulebi Svilebsa da Zmas ganuwesa samkvidrod, gaitaca monastridan qvrivi da gadaixvewa (daubrunda!) gurias Tavisufal axalsaxlobad...
... eses nagrami guriaSi dResacaa da maTi `saginebeli~ saxelia msaxuraiSvilebi (mefis msaxurionidan). `saginebeli~ myarad rCeba gvaris kacs da saSualebacaa sisxlis ar aRrevisa odiTganve.
... eses nagrami pirveli colidan imereTs gamravlda da warmoCndnen umetesad sasuliero da mwignobrul arealze – CxeiZeebi.
... me-18, me-19 saukuneebSi CxaiZeebi xSirad `ixseniebian~ mZafri saTavgadasavlo istoriebiT evropasa da amerikaSi (umetesad, samwuxarod, kriminaluri qronikebidan _ gansakuTrebiT asea amerikaSi, italiaSi da safrangeTSi).
